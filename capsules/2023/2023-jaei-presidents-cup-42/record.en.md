# 42nd President's Cup National Swimming Championships — Jaei Kim: Gold (50m BR), Silver (100m BR)

- **Athlete**: Jaei Kim (4th grade, Cheonggye Elementary School; Gwacheon Youth Training Center master class)
- **Meet**: 42nd President's Cup National Swimming Championships (대통령배 전국수영대회) — one of Korea's premier national meets
- **Dates/Venue**: August 12–17, 2023, Jeonju Wansan Aquatics Center, Korea
- **Results**:
  - 50m Breaststroke — **Gold (1st)**
  - 100m Breaststroke — **Silver (2nd)**

## Context

- Won two bronze medals (50m/100m breaststroke) at the 52nd National Youth Sports Festival in May of the same year.
- Took 2nd in the 100m breaststroke at the Kyobo Life Cup Youth Sports Festival immediately before this meet.
- Maintained 1st–2nd place finishes at national meets for the rest of the season.
