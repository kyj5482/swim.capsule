# Selected for Team Gyeonggi — 53rd National Youth Sports Festival — Jia Kim

- **Athlete**: Jia Kim (4th grade, Cheonggye Elementary School; Gwacheon Youth Training Center master class, coached by Lee Sang-hyun)
- **News**: Three swimmers (four events) from the Gwacheon Youth Training Center master class were selected to represent Gyeonggi Province in swimming at the 53rd National Youth Sports Festival (2024) — Jia among them.
- **Trials (swimming)**: April 4–5, 2024, Ansan Daebu Sports Center pool — the article's "April 4–6" includes the diving trials (Apr 6); confirmed by the Gyeonggi Swimming Federation's official meet notice (`source-gswimming.html`)
- **Selection results**: **2nd place** in the 50m butterfly, plus qualification in the 100m butterfly — entries in two events
- **Main stroke**: Butterfly
- **Selected alongside**: Yoon Jeong-yun (9th grade, 1st in 200m fly) and her brother Jaei Kim (5th grade, 2nd in 100m breaststroke — his second consecutive selection)

## Career notes cited in the article

- 2022 National Community Sports Festival — 3rd in 50m butterfly, 3rd in 50m breaststroke
- 2023 — broke the **Korean national record** in the 50m butterfly (community division)
